Napping Tool / Alarm Clock

Napping is an Excellent Tool to regulate Stress!

Did you know?

  • Naps improve your concentration and ability to learn and memorize new information
  • Napping can help regulate emotions by allowing you some time to step away from stress to rest
  • Taking a short nap after lunch may help reduce your risk of heart disease by decreasing post-meal blood sugar spikes
  • The best time of the day to take a nap is usually between 1 p.m. and 5 p.m.
  • The two most ideal napping durations are 25 minutes and 90 minutes
